### who i am

18-year-old <mark>MBBS</mark> student who spends way too much time thinking about products, systems, and why that API call isn't working. medicine gives me the discipline and the long game. code gives me the leverage. i'm playing both.

somewhere between anatomy lectures and clinical rotations, i'm building streaming platforms, wiring up AI, automating things that shouldn't need a human, and quietly working toward something worth backing — YC, Khosla, Accel, Z47 kind of backing. that's the goal. no sugarcoating it.

### how i think

**<mark>pattern recognition</mark>** is the thing. in code, in <mark>chess</mark>, in a <mark>cricket</mark> match, in a conversation, in a market. once i see the structure of something i can't unsee it — and i'm usually ahead of what's about to happen next.

when i'm locked in, everything else stops. food, time, notifications — all of it goes quiet. the work is the only signal. i don't fight that. it's just how i'm wired.

i listen to a lot of <mark>podcasts</mark> — founders, researchers, investors. not background noise, actually listening. building a mental model of how the people i want to be around think.
